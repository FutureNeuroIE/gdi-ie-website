Welcome to the home page of the Genome of Ireland (GoI) Project!

We aim to create a dataset of DNA that represents the general Irish adult population. The same initiative is being carried out in other European countries. Together, these national projects will form what will be known as the Genome of Europe (GoE). The GoI is also providing Irish data to be hosted by GDI Ireland in the long-term

This project launched officially on 29th January 2025. Updates will be provided on this page



How will Genome of Ireland data be used?


GoI aims to establish a reference whole genome sequence (WGS) dataset of at least 1,200 adults living in Ireland
